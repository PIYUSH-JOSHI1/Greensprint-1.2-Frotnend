Plant & Track Trees

Register trees with QR codes, GPS location, and species info. Monitor growth with AI-powered photo verification.

🤖

AI Tree Verification

YOLOv8-powered detection verifies tree planting photos to ensure authenticity and prevent fraud.

Analytics Dashboard
